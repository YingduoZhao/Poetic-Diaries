My Mother’s 80th Birthday - Kaiserin Elisabeth con österrich



I ask God to graciously send
you some summers as you love him,
Where freshness gives you the sun's rays
And hours you stay in the beech grove.

At night, however, the moon shall turn its face to you,
As you recently described to me with delight.
If nature knows how to bloom so sweetly,
Old age will flee from you for a long time to come!

Ich bitte Gott, Er möge gnädig senden
Dir manchen Sommer noch, wie du ihn liebst,
Wo Frische dir die Sonnenstrahlen spenden
Und Stunden du im Buchenhain verbliebst.

Nachts aber soll der Mond sein Antlitz wenden
Dir zu, wie du entzückt mir jüngst beschriebst.
Wem die Natur so lieblich weiss zu blühen,
Vor dem wird lange noch das Alter fliehen!



Eliabeth's mother, Duchess Ludovika in Bavaria, also known as Princess Ludovika of Bavaria, turned 79 on August 30, 1887, so she had her 80th birthday. Ludovika and her husband had ten children, including Empress Elisabeth of Austria and Queen Maria Sofia of the Two Sicilies. Ludovika was always frustrated that, unlike her elder sisters (who married kings and Austrian archdukes), she would not be marrying someone with a grand title, but rather a peculiar and childish Duke who had a fondness for circuses. Because of this, Ludovika tried hard to let her children to get marry to people who are in higher classes. This is part of the reason why Empress Elisabeth, a girl come from the lower status, had the chance of marrying the Austro-Hungarian Empire Franz Joseph I.
